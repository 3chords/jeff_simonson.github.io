Findings...

Compared to temperature, the patterns in wind speed vs. city latitude are not as apparent. However, northern cities, especially those with a latitude above 30 degrees tend to be more windy than their southern hemisphere counterparts. Again, this could be seasonal - the north is in spring, the south in fall. Of the four scatter plots, the only chart where a relationship exists with latitude is temperature.
